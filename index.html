Who We Are

Extending Hope Through Health & Empowerment

Dr. WaZzz Foundation is a humanitarian nonprofit rooted in Cross River State, Nigeria, reaching communities across Nigeria and Cameroon. We deliver comprehensive health outreach, psychosocial care, nutrition support, and community development programs tailored to the needs of displaced and vulnerable populations.

Guided by public health expertise and community partnerships, we connect refugees, internally displaced persons, and underserved groups with critical medical care, education, and livelihood opportunities. Our mission is to ensure every person can live healthier, empowered, and dignified lives, no matter their circumstance.
