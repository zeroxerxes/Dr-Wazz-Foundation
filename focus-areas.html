Transforming Health & Livelihoods Across Nine Focus Areas

Our multidisciplinary programs integrate health, psychosocial support, emergency relief, and empowerment to address the urgent and long-term needs of refugees, IDPs, and vulnerable host communities.

Explore Programs
Integrated Services

Evidence-Based Programs With Measurable Outcomes

Each focus area is anchored in community insights, rigorous data, and collaborations with government agencies, INGOs, and grassroots leaders.

Public Health & Primary Care

Mobile clinics, community consultations, and referral pathways ensure equitable access to essential services.

Nutrition & Supplementation

Ready-to-use therapeutic foods, micronutrient supplementation, and maternal nutrition counseling.

Mental Health & Psychosocial Support

Survivor-centered counseling, support groups, and psychosocial first aid delivered by trained practitioners.

Women’s & Children’s Health

Antenatal care, immunization support, GBV prevention, and adolescent health education.

Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Hygiene promotion, handwashing stations, menstrual health workshops, and safe water advocacy.

Food & Emergency Relief

Rapid response distributions of food baskets, dignity kits, and transitional shelter materials.

Vocational Training

Skills development, entrepreneurship coaching, and market linkages for displaced youth and women.

Health Communication

Culturally relevant campaigns, radio programs, and community theater for behavior change.

Research & Evidence Generation

Participatory research, surveillance, and policy briefs shaping health and humanitarian programming.

Program Design

Built With Communities, Backed by Data

Rapid Needs Assessments

We co-design interventions using FGDs, household surveys, and participatory ranking.

Capacity Building

Volunteers and refugee-led groups receive ongoing mentorship to manage and scale programs.

Monitoring & Learning

Real-time dashboards, After Action Reviews, and partner learning sessions ensure adaptive management.

Partner With Us to Scale Lifesaving Support

We collaborate with UN agencies, INGOs, donors, and private sector allies to expand coverage and deepen impact. Let's co-create resilient, community-led solutions.
